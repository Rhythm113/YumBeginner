Roasted Chicken

A DELICIOUS AND CRISPY ROASTED CHICKEN RECIPE

About Roasted Chicken

Roasted chicken is a classic dish that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. When prepared properly, the skin becomes beautifully crisp while the meat stays juicy and tender. This recipe uses a blend of aromatic Indian spices that infuse the chicken with incredible flavor. The yogurt in the marinade helps tenderize the meat while creating a delicious crust during roasting.

This version is scaled for individual servings, making it perfect whether you're cooking for one or a crowd. The spices can be adjusted to your taste preferences, and the cooking time may vary slightly depending on your oven and the size of your chicken pieces.

Instructions:

  1. Clean and pat dry the chicken pieces with paper towels.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and all spices to make a marinade.
  3. Rub the marinade all over the chicken pieces, making sure to coat evenly. Marinate for at least 2 hours (overnight for best results).
  4. Preheat oven to 375째F (190째C). Place chicken on a roasting rack in a baking tray.
  5. Roast for about 35-45 minutes (depending on piece size), basting occasionally with the juices, until golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165째F (74째C).
  6. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Serve hot with naan or rice.
