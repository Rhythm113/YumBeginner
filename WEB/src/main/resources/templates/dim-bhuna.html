About Dim Bhuna

Dim Bhuna is a flavorful Bangladeshi-style spicy egg curry where boiled eggs are cooked in a rich, thick masala sauce. This dish is known for its deep, robust flavors and beautiful golden-red color from the spices. The eggs absorb the aromatic spices while maintaining their texture, creating a perfect balance between the creamy yolk and the spicy sauce.

This recipe is perfect for quick meals, as it uses simple ingredients that are usually available in every kitchen. The bhuna technique (slow cooking and stirring) intensifies the flavors and creates a thick, clinging sauce that coats the eggs beautifully. Serve with rice or flatbread for a satisfying meal that's both comforting and packed with protein.

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil and fry chopped onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic and tomato; cook until soft.
  3. Add spices and salt; cook until oil separates.
  4. Add the boiled egg and stir to coat with the masala.
